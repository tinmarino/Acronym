<div class="mw-parser-output"><p>

This is a compilation of initialisms and acronyms commonly used in astronomy. Most are drawn from professional astronomy, and are used quite frequently in scientific publications. A few are frequently used by the general public or by <a href="/wiki/Amateur_astronomy" title="Amateur astronomy">amateur astronomers</a>.

<p>The acronyms listed below were placed into one or more of these categories:
</p>
<ul><li>Astrophysics terminology – <i>physics-related acronyms</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Astronomical_catalog" title="Astronomical catalog">Catalog</a> – <i>collections of tabulated scientific data</i></li>
<li>Communications network – <i>any network that functions primarily to communicate with <a href="/wiki/Spacecraft" title="Spacecraft">spacecraft</a> rather than performing astronomy</i></li>
<li>Data – <i>astrophysical data not associated with any single catalog or observing program</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Astronomical_object" title="Astronomical object">Celestial object</a> – <i>acronyms for natural objects in space and for adjectives applied to objects in space</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Instrumentation" title="Instrumentation">Instrumentation</a> – <i>telescope and other spacecraft equipment, particularly detectors such as imagers and <a href="/wiki/Spectrometer" title="Spectrometer">spectrometers</a></i></li>
<li>Meeting – <i>meetings that are not named after organizations</i></li>
<li>Observing program – <i>astronomical programs, often surveys, performed by one or more individuals; may include the groups that perform surveys</i></li>
<li>Organization – <i>any large private organization, government organization, or company</i></li>
<li>Person – <i>individual people</i></li>
<li>Publication – <i>magazines, scientific journals, and similar astronomy-related publications</i></li>
<li>Software – <i>software excluding catalogued data (which is categorized as  "catalog") and scientific images</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Spacecraft" title="Spacecraft">Spacecraft</a> – <i>any spacecraft except space telescopes</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Telescope" title="Telescope">Telescope</a> – <i>ground-based and space telescopes; organizations that operate telescopes (for example, the <a href="/wiki/National_Optical_Astronomy_Observatory" title="National Optical Astronomy Observatory">National Optical Astronomy Observatory (NOAO)</a>) are listed under "organization"</i></li></ul>
